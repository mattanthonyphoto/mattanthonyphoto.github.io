Case Study

Positioning Summerhill as the Sunshine Coast's Premier Luxury Builder

A complete visual identity partnership across five luxury residential properties, encompassing photography, videography, and aerial.

Scroll
Client
Summerhill Fine Homes
Location
Sunshine Coast, BC
Services
Photo · Video · Aerial
Scope
5 Luxury Residences

The Brief

A builder ready to be seen differently

Summerhill Fine Homes had built an exceptional reputation on the Sunshine Coast through quality craftsmanship and an unwavering attention to detail. But their visual presence didn't yet match the calibre of their work. As they prepared for a brand refresh, updating their website, elevating their social media, and submitting projects for industry awards like the Georgies, they needed a visual partner who could capture the full story of what they build and why it matters.

The brief was clear: create a comprehensive visual library that would position Summerhill as the leading luxury builder on the Sunshine Coast. The imagery needed to feel premium, intentional, and true to the homes themselves. Not staged or artificial, but honest and refined.

The Approach

Working as a creative partner, not just a photographer

This wasn't a single shoot. It was a sustained creative collaboration. Across five distinct luxury residential properties, we worked closely with the Summerhill team to understand each home's design intent, its relationship to the landscape, and what made it uniquely Summerhill.

Every photoshoot was carefully timed to capture the best natural light. Golden hour exteriors, soft diffused interiors, and twilight shots that brought the architecture to life. Drone and aerial photography showed how each property sits within the stunning coastal environment, adding scale and context that ground-level photography alone can't achieve.

Alongside the architectural work, we produced team portraits that reflect Summerhill's culture. Approachable, skilled, and proud of their craft. We also captured lifestyle and construction progression imagery that tells the story behind the finished product. Every frame was considered, and every deliverable was built around supporting Summerhill's long-term brand goals.

Deliverables

A visual library built for every touchpoint

Architectural Photography

Interior and exterior photography across five luxury residential properties, timed for optimal light and composed to honour each home's design.

Videography

Cinematic video content showcasing the flow, scale, and atmosphere of each property, crafted for web and social platforms.

Drone & Aerial

Elevated perspectives showing the relationship between architecture and the Sunshine Coast landscape, adding depth and grandeur.

Team Portraits

Professional headshots and environmental portraits of the Summerhill team, reflecting their approachable expertise and pride in craft.

Lifestyle Photography

Candid, editorial-style imagery capturing the people, process, and culture behind Summerhill's builds.

Progression Shots

Construction documentation from foundation to finish. Visual storytelling that demonstrates the depth of Summerhill's build process.

The Outcome

A brand that now reflects the work

The imagery created through this partnership gave Summerhill the visual foundation they needed to reposition themselves in the market. Their refreshed website now leads with striking, high-end photography that immediately communicates quality and intent. Their social media presence carries a consistent premium aesthetic. And their Georgie Award submissions are backed by imagery that does their craftsmanship justice.

More than individual photos or videos, the outcome was a shift in perception. Summerhill Fine Homes is now visually positioned as one of the leading luxury builders on British Columbia's Sunshine Coast, with the imagery to prove it at every touchpoint.
