Gambier Island Residence

Gambier Island, British Columbia

All Projects
Location
Gambier Island
Designer
Linwood Homes
Builder
Black Thumb Contracting

Craftsmanship and family legacy on a remote, generational property

This Gambier Island residence features soaring wood ceilings, expansive windows, and a natural material palette that connects interiors directly to the surrounding landscape. Built by Hayden Killam of Black Thumb Contracting using pre-packaged Linwood Homes materials delivered by barge and helicopter, the home embodies the logistical ingenuity required for remote island construction.

Every detail reflects the family's deep connection to this generational property, from the wood ceiling structure to the carefully framed forest and ocean views throughout the home.
