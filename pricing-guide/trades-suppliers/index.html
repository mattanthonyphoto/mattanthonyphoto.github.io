Photography for Trades & Suppliers
Photography for Trades & Suppliers

Your craft disappears into buildings. The photography should make it visible

Custom millwork, architectural glazing, structural fabrication, specialty finishes. Your work enables architecture. The right photography documents it before the next project covers it up.

Scroll
Why This Matters

What happens when craft is invisible

Your best work lives inside architect-designed homes and commercial builds. But once the project is complete, you rarely get credited. The builder's photographer shoots wide angles. Your three months of custom fabrication becomes background.

Without photography that isolates and documents your specific contribution, architects and designers evaluating future partners have no way to see what you actually do.

Your Work Gets Credited to Someone Else

When a builder or architect shares project photos, your millwork, windows, or fabrication is visible but unattributed. The architect gets the credit. You get nothing for your portfolio.

Architects Can't Find You

Design professionals evaluate trades by portfolio quality. If your website shows phone shots or supplier stock images, you look like a commodity, not a craft partner.

Product-in-Context is Missing

Isolated product shots on a white background don't show how your work performs in real architecture. Architects want to see installations, integrations, and detail in context.

Award Opportunities Are Lost

Projects you contributed to win Georgie and CHBA awards, but without your own photography of the installation, you can't leverage those wins in your own marketing.

Context Over Isolation

Your work photographed inside the buildings it was made for. Not product shots on a white background, but custom installations in architect-designed spaces where your craft is part of the design story.

Detail at the Right Scale

Macro detail of joinery, material transitions, and fabrication precision alongside wider compositions that show how your work integrates into the architecture. Both scales matter.

Portfolio Assets That Last

Every image is composed for your website, proposals, trade show displays, and architect presentations. One shoot produces years of marketing material across every channel.

Who This Is For

Specialists whose work enables architecture

Millwork & Cabinetry

Custom built-ins, architectural paneling, and bespoke cabinetry documented in the residential and commercial spaces they were fabricated for.

Windows & Glazing

Architectural fenestration, operable glass walls, and custom door systems photographed to show how your engineering integrates with the design.

Structural Fabrication

Custom steel, heavy timber, engineered connections, and mass timber systems. Your precision documented where it's structurally and visually exposed.

Specialty Trades

Stone and tile, lighting design, hardware, flooring, landscape architecture, cladding, and building envelope. Any craft that deserves better documentation than it currently has.

Common Questions

Before you reach out

Do I need to book my own shoot, or can I join an existing one?

Either. Most trades start by joining a project shoot I'm already doing for a builder or architect. You get your own curated set of images focused on your work, at a lower cost. Dedicated shoots are available when you want to document multiple installations or need deeper coverage.

How do I find out about upcoming shoots I can join?

Let me know which builders and architects you work with, or which projects you've recently completed installations on. I'll check whether any of those projects have photography scheduled and coordinate your inclusion.

What if the project has already been photographed?

If I shot the project, I may already have images that feature your work. We can discuss licensing those images directly. If another photographer shot it, a dedicated session to document your installations is the best path.

Can you photograph our work in the shop or during installation?

Yes. Process and fabrication documentation is available as an add-on. It's valuable for social media, recruitment, and showing architects the craft that goes into every piece before it leaves your shop.

Do we get our own images, separate from the builder's?

Yes. Every party in a cost-share arrangement receives their own curated set composed specifically around their contribution. Your images highlight your work. Full unlimited usage rights included.

Can I use the images for trade shows and architect presentations?

Yes. Every engagement includes a full unlimited use license. Website, trade shows, architect presentations, proposals, social media, print. No restrictions.

We work across multiple projects a year. Is there a way to cover them all?

Yes. If you're contributing to 4+ projects annually, a retainer or multi-project arrangement ensures your installations are documented consistently. We can discuss the right structure on a discovery call.

What's the first step?

Book a discovery call. We'll talk about your craft, the projects you've contributed to, and whether a dedicated shoot or cost-share arrangement makes more sense. No pricing at that stage. Just a conversation about fit.
