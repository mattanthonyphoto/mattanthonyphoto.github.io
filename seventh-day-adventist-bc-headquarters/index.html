Featured Project

SDA BC & Yukon Headquarters

Abbotsford, British Columbia

All Projects
Location
Abbotsford
Architect
Sitelines Architecture
Size
25,678 sq ft

Mass timber, twisted beams, and transparent glass creating a regional anchor for faith and service

The headquarters for the Seventh Day Adventist BC and Yukon is a pure expression of Sitelines Architecture's innovative and sustainable design ethos, marked by its use of mass timber and distinctive twisted beams that welcome visitors. These beams not only serve as an architectural focal point but also signify the embrace of community and nature.

The use of natural materials speaks to a commitment to environmental stewardship, while the building's transparent glass allows for a fluid interface with the surrounding landscape. The design's organic forms and warm wood construction create an atmosphere of tranquility and strength, reflective of the organization's values and its role as a regional anchor for faith and service.
