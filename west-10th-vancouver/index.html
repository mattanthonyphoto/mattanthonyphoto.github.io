Contemporary, sleek design that maximizes natural light and expansive urban views

The W 10th project in Vancouver features a contemporary, sleek design that maximizes natural light and provides expansive views of the surrounding urban landscape. The residence utilizes Reynaers' Masterline 8 for the windows, MasterPatio for the sliding doors, and CW 50 for a custom skylight, offering not only beauty but also exceptional thermal efficiency.

The design incorporates large glass panels to create an open, airy atmosphere, while ensuring privacy and energy savings with high-performance systems. This project is a perfect example of urban modernism meeting sustainable living.
