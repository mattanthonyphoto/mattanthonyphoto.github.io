The ROI

One shoot day. Months of content.

Most builders know they need content. Few have the time to create it. That is where the quarterly embedded shoot day changes everything. We show up on an active job site, spend the day documenting the real work in progress, and deliver a library of assets that keeps your marketing running for months.

One shoot produces 8 to 12 Instagram posts, 3 to 5 Reels, 4 to 6 LinkedIn posts, recruitment content, and proposal imagery. No staging, no interruptions, no content calendars gathering dust. Just authentic documentation of the work your team does every day, turned into the most efficient content investment a builder can make.

While your competitors post phone photos and stock images, your feed shows the real work happening on real job sites. That is a competitive advantage that compounds.

Construction Progression

Crew Culture & Lifestyle

The morning huddle. The precision of a crane lift. Your team in their element. The authentic moments that show what it is like to work with your company.

Team Portraits & Headshots

Professional headshots and environmental portraits for your website, LinkedIn profiles, proposals, and recruitment materials. Shot on site, in context.

Short Form Video & Reels

Vertical video content ready for Instagram Reels, LinkedIn, and TikTok. Site walk-throughs, time-lapses, and crew spotlights that stop the scroll.

Drone & Aerial Documentation

Social-Ready Deliverables

Every image and video delivered cropped, colour-graded, and formatted for the platforms you use. No resizing, no guesswork. Ready to post.
